Omah, meaning 'house' in Javanese, reflects our homestay's essence. Chosen to honor our owner's mixed Javanese heritage, it symbolizes warmth, cultural richness, and hospitality. Welcome to Omah, where every guest becomes family.

Our Vision and Mission

Vision:

To become a leading homestay destination known for exceptional hospitality, comfort, and a welcoming atmosphere, ensuring every guest feels at home.

Mission:

To provide a memorable and enriching homestay experience through personalized service, comfortable accommodations, and a commitment to excellence, fostering a sense of belonging for every guest.
